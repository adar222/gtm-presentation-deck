Risks & Mitigation

Potential Risks

  • Lower Funnel ConversionActual close rates are lower than the forecasted 50%, requiring more top-of-funnel activity.
  • Technical DelaysPublisher-side technical hurdles slow down the integration and onboarding process.
  • CPM SeasonalityQ1 is traditionally a softer quarter for ad spend, which could impact initial eCPM performance.

Mitigation Plan

  • Weekly Funnel ChecksMonitor conversion rates in real-time and quickly adjust outreach volume or messaging as needed.
  • Dedicated OnboardingOur Publisher Success Manager will provide hands-on support to resolve issues and accelerate time-to-revenue.
  • Leverage Unique DemandOverwolf's exclusive, gamer-focused demand is less susceptible to broad market seasonality and performs consistently.
