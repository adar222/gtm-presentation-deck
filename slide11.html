Risks & Action Plan

Potential Risks

Lower Funnel Conversion

Actual close rates are lower than the forecasted 50%, requiring more top-of-funnel activity.

Technical Delays

Publisher-side technical hurdles slow down the integration and onboarding process.

Q1 is traditionally a softer quarter for ad spend, which could impact initial eCPM performance.

Action Plan

Weekly Funnel Checks

Monitor conversion rates in real-time and quickly adjust outreach volume or messaging as needed.

Dedicated Onboarding

Our Publisher Success Manager will provide hands-on support to resolve issues and accelerate time-to-revenue.

Leverage Unique Demand

Overwolf's exclusive, gamer-focused demand is less susceptible to broad market seasonality and performs consistently.
