My Hiring Principles

1. Revenue Triggers Growth

We don't hire "just in case." New roles are unlocked only when we hit pre-defined revenue milestones.

2. Every Role Drives Revenue

Each new team member will have a clear mandate to directly accelerate growth and improve our core KPIs.

3. Lean & High-Impact

We will maintain a small, elite team where every individual is a top performer, ensuring maximum efficiency and fiscal responsibility.
